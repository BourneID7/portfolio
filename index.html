About Me

I am a full-stack web developer who makes complex product information understandable with visually appealing, organized, user-friendly websites and graphics. I grasp it, imagine it, and build it.

During my career as senior graphic designer and photographer I’ve built a reputation for on-time delivery of clear and attractive design projects including print advertising, front-end web design, and photo manipulation. I am an expert in Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, and Illustrator, with strong skills in HTML and CSS.

My background is in print design but my passion is web design. I am pursuing a new career as a full-stack web developer, studying at the UW Extension Coding Bootcamp. I’ve earned a front-end web design certificate from MSOE and a bachelor's degree in graphic design from Cardinal Stritch University. My visual design background will be a benefit to future employers looking for a web developer. After all, the goal is a website that works well and looks great too.

On the personal side, I enjoy hot rods and classic cars. I drive a 1927 Ford T-bucket. In summer I frequently combine my love of cars and photography by taking artistic detail photos of the cars at local car shows.
